Airport Security

When international air travelers arrive at airports and check into their flights, their passports are scanned by TSA agents. In many cases, these passport scans are stored in airport databases for months at a time. These scans are huge treasure troves of information that, if obtained unlawfully, could result in tens of thousands of stolen identities.

No effective mechanism has been implemented to ensure that the passenger who checked into the airline is the same one getting on the plane.
